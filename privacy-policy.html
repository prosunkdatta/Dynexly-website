Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Dynexly ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website dynexly.org and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or interact with us through our online forms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information that you voluntarily provide to us. This happens when you:

  • Submit the contact form on our Contact page (name, email, subject, message).
  • Submit an application through the Join page (name, email, age, country, area of interest, GitHub/LinkedIn/portfolio link, and a personal statement).

We do not collect any information automatically through cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising networks. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking technology.

2. How We Use Your Information

The personal data you provide is used solely for the purpose for which you submitted it:

  • Contact form submissions are used to respond to your inquiry.
  • Join form applications are reviewed by our team to evaluate potential volunteer research positions.

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose. Dynexly is a non‑profit research collective; we have no revenue model and do not sell, rent, or trade any personal data.

3. Data Sharing and Third‑Party Services

We use Formspree (formspree.io) to process form submissions. When you submit a form on our website, your information is transmitted to Formspree’s servers and then forwarded to our organizational email address. Formspree’s privacy policy can be found at https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Apart from this necessary service, we do not share your data with any other third party, except as required by law.

4. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. If we ever introduce essential session cookies for website functionality, we will update this policy accordingly.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information submitted through our forms. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. By using our website, you acknowledge that we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our research team consists entirely of individuals aged 14–22. Accordingly, we may collect personal information from minors aged 14 and above through our Join form. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 14. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received information from someone under 14 without proper consent, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at dynexlylabs@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
